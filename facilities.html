Our Facilities

Every facility at St. Andrew SSS is designed with the student's growth, comfort and learning in mind.

Science Laboratories

Three fully equipped laboratories for Physics, Chemistry and Biology. Each lab accommodates 40 students with individual workstations, safety equipment, and modern apparatus for practical experiments aligned with the UNEB syllabus.

Sports Grounds & Courts

Expansive sports facilities including a full-size football pitch, netball courts, athletics track, basketball court, and volleyball court. All maintained to district competition standards with proper lighting for evening practice.

Dormitories

Separate, secure boarding houses for boys and girls with house parents ensuring a safe and comfortable environment.

Medical Room

On-site first aid room staffed by a trained nurse. Emergency protocols in place with access to nearby health facilities.

Dining Hall

Spacious, well-ventilated dining hall serving three meals daily. Nutritional menus reviewed by health professionals.

Chapel / Prayer Space

A dedicated space for prayer, reflection and spiritual growth. Weekly services and daily devotion times for all students.
