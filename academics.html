Our Programs

O-Level & A-Level

Two comprehensive academic programs aligned with the Uganda National Curriculum and UNEB requirements.

O-Level (Senior 1–4)

The Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) program runs over four years, covering a broad curriculum that prepares students for national examinations administered by UNEB.

  • 8 core subjects + 2 electives
  • Practical science and arts activities
  • Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) exam
  • Pathway to A-Level or vocational studies

A-Level (Senior 5–6)

The Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) is our two-year advanced program for students qualifying for university or professional education at degree level.

  • 3 principal subjects + 2 subsidiaries
  • Research papers and presentations
  • Uganda Advanced Certificate (UACE) exam
  • Pathway to Makerere, MUBS and top universities
