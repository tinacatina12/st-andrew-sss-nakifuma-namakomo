School Life

At St. Andrew SSS, we develop self-reliant, disciplined, and productive learners — on the sports field, in clubs, and serving the Namakomo community.

Co-Curricular

Clubs & Activities

Our clubs develop organisation, self-discipline, leadership, and commitment — key life skills aligned with our school values.

Debate Club
45 members

Weekly debates, public speaking training, and inter-school competitions. Builds critical thinking and confidence.

Science Club
38 members

Experiments, science fairs and STEM projects. Students explore biology, chemistry and physics beyond the classroom.

Music, Dance & Drama
60 members

Annual school concerts, cultural celebrations, and national MDD competitions showcasing Ugandan heritage.

Student Guild
12 members

Elected student leaders govern school affairs, organise events, and serve as the voice of the student body.

Community Service
55 members

Environmental clean-ups, hospital visits and charity drives developing empathy and social responsibility.

Spelling Bee
30 members

Annual inter-school spelling competition sharpening vocabulary and language skills at all levels.

Student Governance

The Student Guild Government

Our elected student leaders build governance skills, organise events, and represent the student body.

Guild President

Elected annually by the student body. Leads the school council and represents students to administration.

Guild Secretary

Manages correspondence, minutes and communication for the guild government.

Guild Treasurer

Oversees student activity budgets and ensures full financial accountability.

Prefects

Senior students appointed to uphold discipline and serve as role models for younger students.
