Admission Process

How to Apply

Our five-step process guides you from first enquiry to enrolment.

1
Download Form

Download the official admission form or collect it from our office.

2
Complete Application

Fill in all details accurately and attach certified copies of documents.

3
Submit Application

Submit at the school office or via email with all supporting documents.

4
Interview

Shortlisted candidates are invited for an interview and entrance assessment.

5
Enrolment

Successful applicants receive an admission letter and complete enrolment.

Requirements

What to Bring

  • Certified copy of PLE results (for S1)
  • Certified copy of UCE results (for S5)
  • Certified copy of birth certificate
  • 4 passport-size photographs
  • Transfer certificate from previous school
  • Medical certificate of fitness
  • Parent/guardian national ID copy
  • Completed admission application form
School Fees

Our fees are competitive and structured to keep quality education accessible. Detailed fee structures are available at the school office.
