Scholarship Program

We believe financial barriers should never block a gifted student. St. Andrew SSS offers partial fee waivers and full scholarships for exceptional academic achievers and students from disadvantaged backgrounds.

  • Top UNEB performers: 50–100% tuition waiver
  • Bursaries for financially disadvantaged families
  • Sports and arts talent scholarships available
  • Application assessed term-by-term
