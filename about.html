Who We Are

St. Andrew Senior Secondary School, Namakomo

St. Andrew Senior Secondary School, Namakomo is a centre of academic growth, discipline, and character development. Guided by the motto "Strive for the Future," the school is committed to providing quality holistic education that empowers learners to face both present and future challenges.

We believe in nurturing hardworking, honest, disciplined, and self-reliant students who are prepared to succeed in education and in life. Through strong core values, spiritual growth, leadership training, and practical life skills, St. Andrew SSS creates an environment where every learner can discover their potential and grow into a responsible member of Ugandan society.

Core Values

What We Stand For

Six core values guide the behaviour, attitude, and performance of every learner and staff member at St. Andrew SSS.

Hard Work

Students are encouraged to put full effort into their studies and daily responsibilities. Success at St. Andrew SSS is built on consistent hard work and dedication to excellence.

Discipline

The school promotes order, self-control, and respect for rules and authority. Discipline is the foundation upon which all other achievements are built at St. Andrew SSS.

Sacrifice

Success is built through commitment, patience, and willingness to give effort for a greater goal. Learners are taught to sacrifice short-term comfort for long-term achievement.

Punctuality

Learners are taught the critical importance of being on time and managing time wisely. Punctuality is respected as a sign of commitment, professionalism, and respect for others.

Trustworthiness

Honesty and reliability are key qualities expected from every member of the St. Andrew SSS community — students, teachers, and staff alike, at all times.

Honesty

The school values truthfulness, integrity, and transparency in all activities. Learners are guided to be honest in their work, conduct, and relationships throughout school life.

Our History

Our Journey of Excellence

From humble beginnings to one of Nakifuma's most respected schools — the St. Andrew SSS story.

Founded
School Established — Nakifuma, Namakomo

St. Andrew Senior Secondary School was established in Nakifuma, Namakomo, with a vision to provide quality holistic education to learners in the community, guided by the motto "Strive for the Future."

Early Years
First UNEB Examinations

The first cohort of students sat the Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) examinations, laying the foundation for a strong academic tradition at St. Andrew SSS.

Growth
A-Level Programme Introduced

Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) classes introduced, expanding opportunities for students to pursue higher education at universities across Uganda.

Development
New Science & ICT Facilities

Modern science laboratories and a computer laboratory with internet access were completed, strengthening science and technology education for all learners.

2023
District Academic Champions

St. Andrew SSS topped district UNEB rankings in both O-Level and A-Level, recording the school's best-ever examination results and affirming its position as a centre of excellence.

2024
94% UNEB Pass Rate — Our Best Yet

With a 94% overall pass rate and 156 distinctions, St. Andrew SSS continues to grow in academic excellence, character development, and community impact — still striving for the future.
